Who We Are

Transforming Lives
Across Communities

ISM is a psycho-social support service provider guided by the motto: Passion for God, Compassion for the people, and Action for the world. Our higher calling is to prolong life, give hope, and restore destinies in communities across the region.

Vision

An institute recognised for innovative quality training and learning with regional standing, strategic research and commitment to outreach and services, uplifting individuals and communities.

Mission

To develop all-rounded individuals and critical thinkers for regional leadership and service who can solve problems in an increasingly complex, dynamic and global society by integrating academic excellence and Christian commitment within a caring community.
